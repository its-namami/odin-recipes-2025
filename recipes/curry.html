Japanese Curry

Easy Japanese curry using store-bought curry roux cubes: a comforting and flavorful dish perfect for any day.

Ingredients

  • Water 600 ml
  • Curry Roux Cubes (e.g., Golden Curry) 100g (usually about half a standard box)
  • Onion 1 medium
  • Carrot 1 medium
  • Potato 1 medium
  • Cooking Oil 1 tbsp
  • Cooked Rice For serving

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the onion, carrot, and potato into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the chopped vegetables and saut√© until the onions become translucent.
  3. Add the water to the pot and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the vegetables are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Turn off the heat. Break the curry roux cubes into pieces and add them to the pot. Stir well until the roux is completely dissolved and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Simmer gently for another 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve the Japanese curry hot over a bed of cooked rice. Enjoy!
